## With icons

To add an icon you need to add a `classModifier`, either `hasiconLeft` or `hasiconRight` whether you want the icon on the left of or on the right.


## Circle button with no text

You can make small round buttons, but do not forget to add a `title`, or an `aria-label` to make your button accessible!
